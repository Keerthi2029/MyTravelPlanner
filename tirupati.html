MAKE YOUR TRIP MEMORABLE BY VISITING

  • Trek through the Tirumala Hills

    Embark on an adventurous trek through the scenic Tirumala Hills, discovering breathtaking landscapes and panoramic views.

  • Visit the Sila Thoranam

    Witness the Sila Thoranam, a unique natural rock formation considered to be one of its kind in Asia. Marvel at its geological significance and beauty.

  • Take a trip to the Akasha Ganga Waterfall

    Experience the beauty of Akasha Ganga Waterfall, surrounded by the tranquility of nature and offering a serene atmosphere.

  • Explore the Sri Venkateshwara Wildlife Sanctuary and plan a jungle safari

    Discover the diverse wildlife at Sri Venkateshwara Wildlife Sanctuary and plan an exciting jungle safari to observe animals in their natural habitat.

  • Spend a day at the Chakra Teertham

    Relax and unwind at Chakra Teertham, enjoying the serene surroundings and the peaceful ambiance of this natural destination.
